Orange County

Summary

From June 10 to June 13, I went to Orange County. I went with some of my family members on my dad's side. We stayed in a hotel. My personal family - including me - has their own room. My cousin's family has their own room. We all went to a lot of places at Orange County and unfortunately stayed up late. I ate breakfast in my hotel room.
