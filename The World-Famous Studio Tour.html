The World-Famous Studio Tour

This tour is amazing! It's so cool that my toddler friend was able to go on this ride and survive. I like this ride because 3D parts, the scenery, and the behind-the-scenes. It isn't really kid-friendly because I heard the word hella on some of the clips they show on the TVs. The line contains escalators and I don't like escalators because they stop sometimes.
