Universal Studios

I went to Universal Studios Hollywood on June 11. I went with my family and friends.

TRANSFORMERS™: The Ride-3D

This Transformers ride almost never fails to amaze me. I really like that you wear glasses for the whole ride because that means that you can see the ride in 3D and 3D rides are some of my favorite rides. My toddler friend was able to go on this ride. In fact, it was interesting when he acted like an acrobat when we were in line. It's sad that one of my cousins was not able to see the young acrobat and the cool 3D ride because my cousin had not finished his lunch. The young monkey did accidentally break line chains which reminded me of when I did the same thing five years ago.
