Universal Studios

I went to Universal Studios Hollywood on June 11. I went with my family and friends.

Despicable Me Minion Mayhem

Despicable Me Minion Mayhem was my favorite ride in June 2022. That's because we went on that ride last before exiting. I enjoyed going through what minions have gone through when training. I used to believe the routine body scanner but I now think the body scanner is a joke. I was surprised in June 2019 when I realized that they no longer gave out 3D glasses. Too bad they changed the ride from 3D to 2D.
